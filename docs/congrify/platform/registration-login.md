## [Registration & Login](https://docs.congrify.com/#/Fintech/RegistrationLogin?id=registration-amp-login)
To create a demo account, you can either contact our support team, or directly sign up on our demo portal following the steps below:
  1. Go to , enter your email address and click on “Continue”.

![Alt text](https://docs.congrify.com/Images/RegistrationLogin_1_login.webp)
  2. Check your inbox for the Email Verification, then click on “confirm email”.

**Important:** Check your spam folder if you are not finding the email in your main inbox.
![Alt text](https://docs.congrify.com/Images/RegistrationLogin_2.webp)
  3. Fill in your information details and create a password to proceed with the sign up. 

![Alt text](https://docs.congrify.com/Images/RegistrationLogin_3.webp)
  4. Your account has now been created. To access the platform, click on “proceed to your account”.

![Alt text](https://docs.congrify.com/Images/RegistrationLogin_4.webp)
  5. You now have access to our demo app dashboard and are able to navigate through the different features and functionalities.

![Alt text](https://docs.congrify.com/Images/RegistrationLogin_5.webp)
If you face any technical difficulties, please contact our support team.
