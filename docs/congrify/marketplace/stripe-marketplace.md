## [Congrify on Stripe's App Marketplace](https://docs.congrify.com/#/Marketplaces/StripeMarketplace?id=congrify-on-stripe39s-app-marketplace)
We’re excited to announce that Congrify’s Intelligence platform is now available directly within the Stripe ecosystem. With our new Stripe app, you can unlock powerful payment insights and performance monitoring—without ever leaving your Stripe dashboard.
The integration is secure, seamless, and takes just a few clicks to set up. Once installed, you’ll be able to analyze trends, detect anomalies, and optimize your payment operations with real-time intelligence tailored to your business.
You can try Congrify for free before upgrading to the Basic plan designed specifically for Stripe users. Get started today and turn your Stripe data into actionable insights.
## [What to expect from Congrify's AI Assistant](https://docs.congrify.com/#/Marketplaces/StripeMarketplace?id=what-to-expect-from-congrify39s-ai-assistant)
With Congrify’s AI Payment Assistant, you can:
  * Chat with an AI model tailored to your Stripe data and get instant answers
  * Receive smart interpretations and actionable recommendations on your payment performance
  * Gain insights in seconds—no downloads, no manual analysis required
  * Learn best practices with curated industry knowledge from seasoned payment experts

## [Onboarding flow](https://docs.congrify.com/#/Marketplaces/StripeMarketplace?id=onboarding-flow)
  1. Visit Stripe’s App Marketplace, search for Congrify, and click ‘Continue’ to install and start onboarding with the Congrify Payment Assistant.”

  2. To install the app, simply allow Congrify secure access to your Stripe account. This ensures a safe and seamless setup.

![Alt text](https://docs.congrify.com/Images/stripe_marketplace_install_step2.png)
![Alt text](https://docs.congrify.com/Images/stripe_marketplace_install_step2_1.png)
  3. After installation, you’ll see a confirmation screen letting you know the setup was successful.

![Alt text](https://docs.congrify.com/Images/stripe_marketplace_install_step3.png)
![Alt text](https://docs.congrify.com/Images/stripe_marketplace_install_step3_1.png)
  4. When you return to the app, you’ll see data syncing in progress. We’ll securely import the last 30 days of your Stripe data, which may take up to 15–30 minutes.

![Alt text](https://docs.congrify.com/Images/stripe_marketplace_install_data_download.png)
  5. After synchronization finishes, the app will display two tabs: Your Insights and AI Assistant.

![Alt text](https://docs.congrify.com/Images//stripe_marketplace_install_using1.png)
  6. Click any item in Your Insights to get an AI-powered analysis of your opportunities, complete with visual charts and a detailed explanation.

![Alt text](https://docs.congrify.com/Images//stripe_marketplace_install_using2.png)
  7. Open the AI Assistant tab to chat with Congrify’s AI and ask questions about your payment performance on Stripe.

![Alt text](https://docs.congrify.com/Images//stripe_marketplace_install_using3.png)
  8. The AI Assistant offers a limited number of interactions before guiding you to continue your analysis in Congrify’s portal. After the initial app registration, click Complete Registration to finish setting up your account.

![Alt text](https://docs.congrify.com/Images//stripe_marketplace_install_using4.png)
  9. To finish registration, simply enter your first and last name and create a password for Congrify’s web portal. Once complete, you can access the portal anytime—either through the Stripe app or directly at app.congrify.com.

![Alt text](https://docs.congrify.com/Images/congrify_payment_assistant_stripe_marketplace_17.png)
  10. Once registered, you’ll have full access to Congrify’s portal, where you can continue using the AI Co-Pilot to analyze your Stripe data and receive personalized recommendations to optimize payment performance. Here, you can also dive into Congrify’s advanced observability and intelligence tools—including extensive analytics, real-time alerting, data exploration, and monitoring capabilities. Simply choose the plan that best fits your business needs.

![Alt text](https://docs.congrify.com/Images/congrify_payment_assistant_stripe_marketplace_19.png)
## [Uninstalling flow](https://docs.congrify.com/#/Marketplaces/StripeMarketplace?id=uninstalling-flow)
  1. Click the three dots next to the AI Payment Assistant app name and select View App Settings.

![Alt text](https://docs.congrify.com/Images/stripe_marketplace_install_uninstalling1.png)
  2. Click Uninstall to remove the app.

![Alt text](https://docs.congrify.com/Images/stripe_marketplace_install_uninstalling2.png)
  3. In the pop-up menu, confirm by clicking Uninstall again.

![Alt text](https://docs.congrify.com/Images/stripe_marketplace_install_uninstalling3.png)
![Alt text](https://docs.congrify.com/Images/stripe_marketplace_install_uninstalling4.png)
  4. A confirmation message will appear once the app is uninstalled.

![Alt text](https://docs.congrify.com/Images/stripe_marketplace_install_uninstalling5.png)
  5. If you’ve created an account in the Congrify portal, contact our support team at support@congrify.com to request its deletion.
