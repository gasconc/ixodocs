## [Satispay](https://docs.congrify.com/#/Integrations/satispay?id=satispay)
## [About Satispay](https://docs.congrify.com/#/Integrations/satispay?id=about-satispay)
Satispay () is a mobile payment platform that enables users to make secure and convenient transactions without a credit or debit card. It operates through a direct connection to the user's bank account, allowing for payments in stores, online purchases, and peer-to-peer transfers. With a focus on simplicity and low transaction fees, Satispay is widely used in Italy and expanding across Europe as a popular alternative to traditional payment methods.
## [How to connect with Satispay](https://docs.congrify.com/#/Integrations/satispay?id=how-to-connect-with-satispay)
To connect with Satispay you would need to obtain their API Key credentials. The process is specified on their online documentation .
## [Required access credentials](https://docs.congrify.com/#/Integrations/satispay?id=required-access-credentials)
  1. Name Name of Satispay's Account 
  2. Key ID The KeyID used for connection to Satispay
  3. Private Key The contents of the private key used for authentication when connecting Satispay 

## [Importing Historical Data](https://docs.congrify.com/#/Integrations/satispay?id=importing-historical-data)
During the onboarding process with Congrify, you can import historical data from Satispay. Please reach out to Congrify's team to confirm which data can be historically downloaded.
