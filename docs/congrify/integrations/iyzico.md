## [izyco(PayU)](https://docs.congrify.com/#/Integrations/iyzico?id=izycopayu)
## [About Izyco (PayU)](https://docs.congrify.com/#/Integrations/iyzico?id=about-izyco-payu)
Izyco (payu) () is a Turkey-based payment service provider that offers secure and seamless payment solutions for businesses and e-commerce platforms. It supports multiple payment methods, including credit/debit cards, installment payments, and local Turkish banking options. Acquired by PayU, iyzico enables businesses to accept online payments with easy integration and fraud protection, making it a key player in the Turkish fintech ecosystem.
## [How to connect with Izyco](https://docs.congrify.com/#/Integrations/iyzico?id=how-to-connect-with-izyco)
To connect with Izyco you would need to obtain their SFTP credentails, please contact Izyco's support.
## [Required access credentials](https://docs.congrify.com/#/Integrations/iyzico?id=required-access-credentials)
  1. Name The name of Izyco's Account
  2. Username The Username of Izyco's SFTP folder.
  3. Password The Password of Izyco's SFTP folder.

## [Importing Historical Data](https://docs.congrify.com/#/Integrations/iyzico?id=importing-historical-data)
During the onboarding process with Congrify, you can import historical data from Izyco. Please reach out to Congrify's team to confirm which data can be historically downloaded.
## [Available Reports](https://docs.congrify.com/#/Integrations/iyzico?id=available-reports)
The following reports are available for the Izyco integration and will provide the necessary data for a successful integration with Congrify. Please ensure that you select these reports as a minimum when configuring your Izyco connection:
  * Balance report
  * Cutoff Report
  * Dispute Report
  * Invoice Report
  * Settlement Report
  * Transaction Report
