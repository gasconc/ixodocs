## [Pre-Chargeback Alerts](https://docs.congrify.com/#/Pre%20Chargeback%20notifications/pre_chargebacks?id=pre-chargeback-alerts)
## [About Congrify's Pre Chargeback Alerts](https://docs.congrify.com/#/Pre%20Chargeback%20notifications/pre_chargebacks?id=about-congrify39s-pre-chargeback-alerts)
Ethoca and Verifi services are seamlessly integrated into Congrify’s Payments Intelligence Platform to proactively detect and manage chargebacks and fraud. Through Ethoca Alerts and Verifi’s Rapid Dispute Resolution(RDR), merchants gain real-time visibility into disputes, enabling early resolution before a chargeback occurs. This integration empowers Congrify users to minimize revenue loss, reduce operational workload, and improve customer experience with actionable intelligence embedded directly into their payment analytics workflows.
## [How to Start with Congrify's Pre Chargeback Alerts](https://docs.congrify.com/#/Pre%20Chargeback%20notifications/pre_chargebacks?id=how-to-start-with-congrify39s-pre-chargeback-alerts)
Getting started is simple. To activate Ethoca and Verifi services within Congrify’s Payments Intelligence Platform, you’ll need just a few key details. See below:
For Ethoca, just provide your merchant descriptor.
For RDR you would need to provide: 
  * your merchant descriptor
  * your acquirer BIN
  * your CAID or MID numbers

With this information on hand, setup is quick and seamless, allowing you to unlock real-time dispute intelligence and chargeback prevention in no time.
## [How to handle the alerts](https://docs.congrify.com/#/Pre%20Chargeback%20notifications/pre_chargebacks?id=how-to-handle-the-alerts)
Handling alerts is flexible and tailored to your workflow.
  * For Ethoca alerts, it’s essential to provide feedback within 24 hours to confirm that a refund has been initiated. 
  * With RDR (Rapid Dispute Resolution), refunds are automatically processed, but you can still send a confirmation back to Congrify for tracking purposes. 

Alerts are delivered via email or Webhooks, and you can respond either through API integration or by logging into the Congrify portal to manage each case manually. This ensures timely resolution and optimal control over dispute handling.
## [How to setup congrify webhooks and API endpoints](https://docs.congrify.com/#/Pre%20Chargeback%20notifications/pre_chargebacks?id=how-to-setup-congrify-webhooks-and-api-endpoints)
To configure Congrify Webhooks and API endpoints, please contact our support team for guidance and access to the technical specifications. Our team will assist you with the setup process to ensure smooth integration and reliable alert handling tailored to your systems.
