# Registration & Login
To create a demo account, you can either contact the support team, or directly sign up on the demo portal following the steps below:
  1. Go to , enter your email address and click on “Continue”.
![](https://documentation.ixopay.com/modules/assets/images/RegistrationLogin_1_login-8e2e4b07c1956c566ad752430ac22800.webp)
  2. Check your inbox for the Email Verification, then click on “confirm email”.
**Important:** Check your spam folder if you are not finding the email in your main inbox.
![](https://documentation.ixopay.com/modules/assets/images/RegistrationLogin_2-51e59ea1e0c02a0f78f7c1fcc3e1ad4e.webp)
  3. Fill in your information details and create a password to proceed with the sign up.
![](https://documentation.ixopay.com/modules/assets/images/RegistrationLogin_3-99dc2e26cea0c792d06e92c4ed99f945.webp)
  4. Your account has now been created. To access the platform, click on “proceed to your account”.
![](https://documentation.ixopay.com/modules/assets/images/RegistrationLogin_4-0c276285e04112bdd590990d0ca2ac71.webp)
  5. You now have access to the demo app dashboard and are able to navigate through the different features and functionalities.
![](https://documentation.ixopay.com/modules/assets/images/RegistrationLogin_5-816268807f80c10b9278163a7ea22d84.webp)
If you face any technical difficulties, please contact the support team.
