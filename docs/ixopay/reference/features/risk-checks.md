# Risk checks
Risk management is a fundamental aspect of any transaction process. To help you safeguard your operations, [IXOPAY platform](https://www.ixopay.com) has a robust set of risk checks designed to identify and prevent potential threats. This ranges from integrated checks within IXOPAY platform to leveraging advanced external risk management systems.
In this section, you will find a wealth of information regarding these risk checks, from setup and configuration guides to best practices and advice. Whether you're implementing basic risk checks or exploring advanced options, these resources will assist you in creating a secure and reliable transaction environment.
