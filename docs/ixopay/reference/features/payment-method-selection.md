# Payment method selection
The payment method selection feature in [IXOPAY](https://www.ixopay.com) enables customers to select their desired payment method during the checkout process. This functionality can contribute to a more user-friendly payment experience by giving customers the flexibility to choose their preferred method of payment.
To customize the payment method selection feature as per your specific requirements, you can refer to the [User Manual](https://docs.ixopay.com/en/platform-user-administration-manual/connector/multi-method-connector/payment-selection-page#Customize) provided by IXOPAY.
