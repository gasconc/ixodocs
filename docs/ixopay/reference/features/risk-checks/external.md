# External risk checks
Ensuring the security of your transactions is paramount. To enhance the security of your operations, [IXOPAY platform](https://www.ixopay.com) integrates with leading external risk management providers. These platforms provide advanced features that help detect and prevent fraudulent activities.
Here, you'll find guides and documentation detailing the process of setting up these external risk checks for your transactions. These guides cover the specific parameters you'll need to include in your transaction `extraData`, initializing risk scripts, and other necessary steps to enable and optimize the use of these risk checks.
