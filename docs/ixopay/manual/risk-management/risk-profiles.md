# Risk Profiles & Risk Rules
In this section of the manual, you’ll define and manage risk profiles and rules—configure screenings, thresholds, and decisioning to reduce fraud while preserving approval rates across your [IXOPAY platform](https://www.ixopay.com) payment flows.
## [ Overview IXOPAY Risk Management Engine enables teams to set up risk profiles and rules to detect fraud, trigger reviews, and block suspicious transactions.](https://documentation.ixopay.com/manual/docs/risk-management/risk-profiles/overview)## [ Available Risk Rules Reference all available risk rules including transaction count, velocity, BIN checks, customer data, and payment method screening.](https://documentation.ixopay.com/manual/docs/risk-management/risk-profiles/risk-rules)## [ Create Risk Profile Create a new risk profile by naming it and configuring rules, score cards, and thresholds before assigning it to merchants or connectors.](https://documentation.ixopay.com/manual/docs/risk-management/risk-profiles/create)## [ Create Risk Rules for Your Risk Profile Add and configure risk rules in risk profiles: transaction count, velocity, amount, country, and customer checks, plus external risk integrations.](https://documentation.ixopay.com/manual/docs/risk-management/risk-profiles/create-risk-rules)## [ Score Card & Actions Define score card actions per risk profile to trigger approvals, declines, or reviews based on numeric risk scores assigned to each rule.](https://documentation.ixopay.com/manual/docs/risk-management/risk-profiles/score-card-actions)## [ Assign Risk Profiles Assign risk profiles to merchants, connectors, meta-connectors, and multi-method connectors to enforce fraud screening at each transaction entry point.](https://documentation.ixopay.com/manual/docs/risk-management/risk-profiles/assign)## [ Risk Statistics Use risk statistics to monitor risk profile performance, review rule hits, and optimize your fraud management strategy.](https://documentation.ixopay.com/manual/docs/risk-management/risk-profiles/risk-statistics)
