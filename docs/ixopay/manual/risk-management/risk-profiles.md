# Risk Profiles & Risk Rules
In this section of the manual, you’ll define and manage risk profiles and rules—configure screenings, thresholds, and decisioning to reduce fraud while preserving approval rates across your [IXOPAY platform](https://www.ixopay.com) payment flows.
