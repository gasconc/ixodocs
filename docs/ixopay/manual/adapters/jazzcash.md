# Jazzcash
Adapter Jazzcash
The **Jazzcash adapter** is integrated, but has not been actively used for an extended period, or could not undergo thorough testing. Since using some capabilities might require further development, we encourage you to reach out directly to your Customer Success Manager or contact our Support Team in the [IXOPAY Customer Experience Portal](https://ixopay.my.site.com/support).
## Jazzcash[​](https://documentation.ixopay.com/manual/adapters/jazzcash#jazzcash-1 "Direct link to Jazzcash")
Configure the following parameters for the Connector (see Connector Configuration - Jazzcash - Generic):
  1. Fill in the **Username**
  2. Fill in the **Password**
  3. Fill in the **API Secret**
