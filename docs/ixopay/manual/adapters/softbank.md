# Softbank
## Adapter Configuration[​](https://documentation.ixopay.com/manual/adapters/softbank#adapter-configuration "Direct link to Adapter Configuration")
Configure the following parameters for the Connector (see Connector Config - Softbank QR Payments):
  1. Fill in the mandatory **Preauthorize Merchant ID**
  2. Fill in the mandatory **Preauthorize Hash Key**
  3. Fill in the mandatory **Extra Data: serviceID**
  4. Fill in the mandatory **Extra Data: customerCode**
  5. Fill in the mandatory **Extra Data: username**
  6. Fill in the mandatory **Extra Data: password**
  7. Fill in the mandatory **Extra Data: debitMerchantI**
  8. Fill in the mandatory **Extra Data: debitHash**
  9. Fill in the mandatory **Extra Data: debitServiceID**
  10. Fill in the mandatory **Extra Data: debitUsername**
  11. Fill in the mandatory **Extra Data: debitPassword**
