# Merchant
Merchants in the [IXOPAY platform](https://www.ixopay.com) are a central entity, they are the actual operators of a sales channel, selling goods or services to customers and processing transactions via the Platform. Dependent on the permission, the Merchant Overview enables clients to create and edit Merchants and associated configuration elements such as Connectors and Users.
