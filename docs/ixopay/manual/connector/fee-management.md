# Fee Management
Our Fee Management Engine enables Users easily to manage and visualize their transactional costs. This feature is especially valuable for our White Label clients who need to manage and charge transactional and periodic fees to their respective customers (Merchants).
The Fee Management Engine configuration is a two step process:
  1. [Create Fee Entities](https://documentation.ixopay.com/manual/docs/connector/fee-management/fee-entities) on (Sub-) Tenant level
  2. [Set up transactional and/or periodic fees](https://documentation.ixopay.com/manual/docs/connector/fee-management/set-up-fees) per Fee Entity on Merchant / Connector level.
