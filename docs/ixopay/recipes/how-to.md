Here, you can find a list of all the available recipes for using the [IXOPAY platform](https://www.ixopay.com). Our recipes provide simple step-by-step guides to help you accomplish common merchant use-cases. Each recipe links to detailed pages in the Guides and Reference sections of our documentation hub, where you can find more information and customize the solutions to fit your specific needs. Whether you're new to IXOPAY platform or an experienced user, our recipes will help you get started quickly and efficiently.
