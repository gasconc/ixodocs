# Introduction
Welcome to the [IXOPAY platform](https://www.ixopay.com) integration guide. This guide is designed to help developers understand how to integrate the IXOPAY platform into their online payment systems.
## Structure of this guide[​](https://documentation.ixopay.com/docs/guides#structure-of-this-guide "Direct link to Structure of this guide")
This documentation is organized into the following sections:
  * [Getting started](https://documentation.ixopay.com/docs/guides/getting-started)
  * [Payments](https://documentation.ixopay.com/docs/guides/payments)
  * [Getting production ready](https://documentation.ixopay.com/docs/guides/production)

Each section contains detailed information on specific topics related to integrating the IXOPAY platform into your website or online platform.
## Who is this documentation for?[​](https://documentation.ixopay.com/docs/guides#who-is-this-documentation-for "Direct link to Who is this documentation for?")
This documentation is intended for developers who are new to online payments and are looking for a reliable payment processing platform to integrate into their website or online platform.
Whether you are developing an online store, a subscription-based service, or any other type of online payment system, this documentation will help you get started with the IXOPAY platform.
## Overview of the platform[​](https://documentation.ixopay.com/docs/guides#overview-of-the-platform "Direct link to Overview of the platform")
⁮IXOPAY platform is a payment processing platform that allows merchants to securely process online payments. It is PCI certified and can tokenize credit card data, making it a secure and reliable option for processing online payments.
The IXOPAY platform offers several [transaction types](https://documentation.ixopay.com/docs/reference/concepts/transactions/types), including debit, preauthorize, capture, void, refund, payout, register, and deregister. It also exposes its functionality via a REST [Transaction API](https://documentation.ixopay.com/api/transaction/transaction-api).

## Getting Started[​](https://documentation.ixopay.com/docs/guides#getting-started "Direct link to Getting Started")
To get started with IXOPAY platform, we recommend following the [Getting started](https://documentation.ixopay.com/docs/guides/getting-started) section. This section will walk you through the process of setting up your account, configuring your payment settings, and integrating IXOPAY platform into your website or online platform.
If you are already familiar with the basics of online payment processing and are looking to customize your payment flow or troubleshoot any issues, we recommend checking out the [Reference](https://documentation.ixopay.com/docs/reference) and [API](https://documentation.ixopay.com/api) sections.
## Additional Resources[​](https://documentation.ixopay.com/docs/guides#additional-resources "Direct link to Additional Resources")
For more information on IXOPAY platform, including pricing, support options, and additional features, please visit our website at [www.ixopay.com](https://www.ixopay.com).
You can find information about payment adapters supported by IXOPAY platform on [adapters.ixopay.com](https://adapters.ixopay.com). The user manual containing explanations on how to use the admin interface can be found at [docs.ixopay.com](https://docs.ixopay.com).
