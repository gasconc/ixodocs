# Getting started
To get started with [IXOPAY platform](https://www.ixopay.com), we recommend following this guide. This section will walk you through the process of setting up your account, configuring your payment settings, and integrating IXOPAY platform into your website or online platform.
If you are already familiar with the basics of online payment processing and are looking to customize your payment flow or troubleshoot any issues, we recommend checking out the [Reference](https://documentation.ixopay.com/docs/reference) and [API](https://documentation.ixopay.com/api) sections.
## Structure of this guide[​](https://documentation.ixopay.com/docs/guides/getting-started#structure-of-this-guide "Direct link to Structure of this guide")
This documentation is organized into the following sections:
  * [Setup your account](https://documentation.ixopay.com/docs/guides/getting-started/setup)
  * [Accept Payments](https://documentation.ixopay.com/docs/guides/getting-started/accept-payments)
  * [Testing your setup](https://documentation.ixopay.com/docs/guides/getting-started/testing)
  * [Callbacks](https://documentation.ixopay.com/docs/guides/getting-started/callbacks)
  * [Recurring payments](https://documentation.ixopay.com/docs/guides/getting-started/recurring-payments)

Each section contains detailed information on specific topics related to integrating IXOPAY platform into your website or online platform.
