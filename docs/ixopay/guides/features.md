# Features
In this section of the documentation, you will find detailed guides on the additional features the [IXOPAY platform](https://www.ixopay.com) provides. These features extend beyond basic payment processing tasks, offering you more flexibility and control over your payment operations.
Each guide in this section is dedicated to a specific feature, providing an in-depth explanation of its functionality, implementation, and benefits. These guides are designed to help you understand how you can leverage these features to enhance your payment process and meet your specific business requirements.
## [ 3-D Secure Enable 3-D Secure on card transactions to meet SCA requirements, reduce fraud, and shift chargeback liability to card issuers.](https://documentation.ixopay.com/docs/guides/features/3d-secure)## [ Customer profiles Store customer payment instruments once and reuse them for merchant-initiated or card-on-file transactions across multiple connectors.](https://documentation.ixopay.com/docs/guides/features/customer-profiles)## [ Pay-by-Link Collect payments by sending customers a secure link via email or SMS, without requiring a checkout page or direct website integration.](https://documentation.ixopay.com/docs/guides/features/pay-by-link)## [ Tokenization & PCI Use single-use tokens for new payments and multiple-use tokens for returning customers to tokenize card data and reduce PCI scope.](https://documentation.ixopay.com/docs/guides/features/tokenization)
By understanding and effectively utilizing these features, you can tailor your payment process to better serve your business needs and provide an optimal experience for your customers.
